# World Layoffs Data Analysis

## Project Structure
- `data_cleaning.sql` — Raw data cleaning (Step 1)
- `exploratory_analysis.sql` — EDA and business insights (Step 2)

## Project Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on global tech layoffs 
from March 2020 to March 2023.

## Business Questions Answered
- Which companies laid off the most employees?
- Which industries were hit hardest?
- Which countries had the most layoffs?
- How did layoffs trend month by month?
- Which companies dominated layoffs each year?

## Key Findings
- 116 companies laid off 100% of their staff
- Amazon, Google and Meta had the highest total layoffs
- 2022 was the worst year for layoffs
- The United States accounted for the majority of layoffs
- Consumer and Retail industries were hit hardest

## Tools Used
- MySQL Workbench
- Dataset: World Layoffs (Kaggle)
